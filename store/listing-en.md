# Chrome Web Store Listing — English

## Name
Tab Manager — Side Panel

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Organize all your tabs by domain in a side panel. Search, group, close old tabs, and manage multiple windows — all in one place.

## Detailed Description

Tired of drowning in browser tabs? Tab Manager gives you a clean, organized overview of every open tab — right in Chrome's side panel.

**Organize by Domain**
All your tabs are automatically grouped by website domain, sorted by most recently accessed. Find what you need instantly.

**Powerful Tab Groups**
Create native Chrome tab groups with one click. Each domain gets its own color-coded group that works across all your windows.

**Search & Filter**
Quickly find any tab by title or URL. Filter by window to focus on what matters.

**Smart Cleanup**
Close individual tabs, all tabs of a domain, or automatically close tabs older than 7 days to keep things tidy.

**Multi-Window Support**
See tabs from all Chrome windows in one unified view, or group them by window — your choice.

**Designed for You**
• Light & dark theme (follows your system automatically)
• 4 languages: English, German, French, Spanish
• Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl+M / Cmd+M
• Quick-open banner on the page edge
• Works in incognito mode

**Privacy First**
No data collection, no tracking, no external servers. Everything runs locally in your browser.
